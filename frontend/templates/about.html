About OncoDeepMind

Developed By: Rikhil Amonkar

OncoDeepMind predicts drug response effectiveness in cancer treatment using advanced AI algorithms, empowering oncologists to make data-driven decisions for personalized patient care. OncoDeepMind was developed to assist oncologists in predicting the effectiveness of drug responses in cancer treatment. Large datasets of patient data have been used to train advanced AI algorithms, enabling the model to analyze complex patterns and relationships within the data. Features have been engineered to help predict the risk of cancer for patients as well, by the filling of a simple form with their details. The model is designed to empower oncologists as well as general users.
