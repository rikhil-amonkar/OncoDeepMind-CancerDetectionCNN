What is OncoDeepMind?

OncoDeepMind is a deep learning-based oncology assistant designed to predict drug response in cancer treatment. By leveraging advanced AI algorithms, it empowers oncologists to make data-driven decisions for personalized patient care. OncoDeepMind is not just tailored to healthcare professionals, but patients as well. Our advances algorithms allow users to enter their own information for a accuraate predicition of their conditions, including risk of cancer. Try OncoDeepMind today!
